CHICKEN MOMO

Steamed dumpling filled with juicy chicken

Description: Momo is a type of steamed dumpling with some form of filling. Momo has become a traditional delicacy in Nepal, Tibet, as well as among Nepalese and Tibetan communities in Bhutan, as well as people of the Indian regions of Darjeeling, Ladakh, Sikkim, Assam, Uttarakhand, Himachal Pradesh and Arunachal Pradesh. In Pakistan, Gilgit-Baltistan region it is known as mamtoo.

Ingredients:

  • 450 grams all purpose flour
  • 300 grams chicken
  • 2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon refined oil
  • 2 large onion
  • 1 tablespoon chilli garlic paste
  • black pepper As required
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper
  • 5 green chilli

Recipe Instruction:

  1. Chop the vegetables and wash chicken
  2. Boil the chicken and then shred well
  3. Prepare the dough and filling for Chicken Momos
  4. Flatten the dough balls and fill it with chicken stuffing, and steam for 20 minutes
